Tabz key bindings

Sequences are leader + one or two keys, vim-style. Allowed keys: a-z, A-Z, 0, $, comma, period, semicolon. Digits 1-9 are reserved for count prefixes, and the leader may not be 0. No binding may equal or start another binding (the shorter one would always fire first). Bindings are not checked against other extensions (Vimium included); avoiding conflicts is up to you.
